About Us

You're In LUCK! The Party Zone is approved with Parks and Recreation as a Vendor for all City of Pueblo Park locations.

1. Contact Parks and Recreation at 719-553-2790 to schedule the usage of an approved park location and to recieve your bounce house permit number*. Make sure you verify if the location you chose has a power source. If not, The Party Zone has generators availale for an additional charge.
2.Once you have recieved your scheduled permit date, time, and location, contact The Party Zone at 719-404-9000 to book the available bounce house of your choice.**

*You must have a bounce house permit to set up a bounce house in any city park.

**Bounce House rental price includes Delivery,Set up, and Take Down of the unit. When renting at a park, the responsible rental party MUST remain in supervision of the Bounce House unit at all times until it is picked up by a Party Zone employee.
